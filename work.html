Mindful Diagnosis: Chief Technology Officer

Mindful Diagnosis is a non-profit business founded by me and 2 other individuals. Our innovative software application utilizes cutting-edge data-driven models to create an immersive and comprehensive education platform for physicians and medical students. By addressing the crucial issue of bias in treatment analysis of patients, Mindful Diagnosis aims to empower healthcare professionals with the knowledge and tools necessary to provide equitable and unbiased care through implicit bias testing. Through interactive modules, case studies, and real-world simulations, our platform equips learners with the skills to mitigate bias, enhance diagnostic accuracy, and improve patient outcomes.
